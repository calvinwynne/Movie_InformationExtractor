Sathi (known also as Saathi, Sathee or Saathee) (English: Companion) is a 2002 Indian Bengali romantic musical drama film directed by Haranath Chakraborty. The film stars Jeet and Priyanka Trivedi. This film is Jeet's cinematic debut. It is the Bengali remake of the 1999 Tamil film Thulladha Manamum Thullum. The film became the highest grossing Bengali films of that year with a box office collection of Rs.9.80 crore.
